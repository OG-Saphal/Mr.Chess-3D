Mr. Chess 3D

Authentic chess, modern multiplayer, and experimental modes in a premium 3D environment.

Unfinished Apr 2021 - Feb 2023

1. Project Overview

Mr. Chess 3D is a Strategy/Board game that combines authentic chess rules with modern multiplayer, social features, and experimental gameplay modes.

2. Core Design Pillars

Authentic Chess Rules

  • Fully compliant with standard chess mechanics
  • Accurate move validation
  • Check, checkmate, and special rules implemented

Modern Multiplayer & Social Play

  • Real-time online matches
  • Integrated voice, chat, and friends system

Experimental Gameplay Without Breaking Chess

  • Blind Chess mode introduces innovation
  • Core chess logic remains intact

Premium 3D Presentation

  • Realistic visuals
  • Smooth camera and animations

3. Gameplay Overview

Primary Objective

Defeat the opponent by delivering checkmate under standard chess rules.

Win Conditions

  • Checkmate
  • Opponent resignation
  • Opponent disconnection (online)

Loss Conditions

  • Checkmate
  • Player resignation
  • Disconnection or timeout

4. Game Modes

Offline Local Multiplayer

  • Two players on the same device
  • Turn-based gameplay
  • Ideal for casual and learning-focused sessions

Online Multiplayer (Photon PUN2)

Match Type:

  • 1v1 real-time chess
  • No observers

Lobby Features:

  • Create / Join rooms
  • Public and private rooms
  • Friend invitations
  • Team / color switching
  • Leave room handling

Networking Focus:

  • Deterministic move synchronization
  • Server-authoritative validation
  • Stable reconnection and disconnect handling

Blind Chess Mode (Experimental)

Concept:

Blind Chess challenges players to rely on memory, prediction, and strategic awareness rather than full board visibility.

Visibility Rule:

  • Only opponent pieces within legal moves are visible
  • All other opponent pieces remain hidden

Design Intent:

  • Introduce a unique chess variant
  • Increase cognitive challenge
  • Demonstrate flexible rule-based logic

5. Player Interaction & Controls

Piece Interaction
